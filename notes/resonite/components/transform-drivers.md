# Transform/Drivers Components

Components registered under the `Transform/Drivers` category in FrooxEngine.
Generic arity is rendered with `<T>` notation.

## User-following subset

These are the most relevant for user tracking or facing:

- `FrooxEngine.AutoLookAtUser`
- `FrooxEngine.CopyGlobalScale`
- `FrooxEngine.CopyGlobalTransform`
- `FrooxEngine.FlipAtUser`
- `FrooxEngine.LookAt`
- `FrooxEngine.LookAtUser`
- `FrooxEngine.PositionAtUser`
- `FrooxEngine.ReferenceUserOverride<T>`
- `FrooxEngine.UserDistanceValueDriver<T>`
- `FrooxEngine.UserInterfacePositioner`
- `FrooxEngine.UserRefToSyncRefAdapter`
- `FrooxEngine.ValueUserOverride<T>`
- `FrooxEngine.VirtualParent`

See `notes/resonite/patterns/user-following.md` for verified usage.
